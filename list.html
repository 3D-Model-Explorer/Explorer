🖼️ Some of the 3D models come from Sketchfab and are used under special licenses — kind of like borrowing cool toys to show your friends!

It's a fun way to build cars, spaceships, characters, and anything you can dream of — all using digital clay! 🪐🎨💻

❓ Help

🚀✨ Welcome to the Awesome 3D Model Explorer! ✨🚀

🎉 Ready to explore some super cool 3D models? Just pick one from the magic list on the left! It’ll appear like magic in the big viewer — and guess what? You can spin it, zoom it, and move it around like a real 3D master! 🧙‍♂️🧠

🎮 How to Move Your Model Around

Use your mouse or trackpad to control the model like a pro! Here's how:

  • 🔄 Spin it Around (Rotate): Click and drag with your mouse to spin the model and check it out from every direction — even from the top and bottom!
  • 🔍 Zoom In and Out: Roll your mouse wheel or slide on your trackpad to zoom super close or zoom out to see the whole thing.
  • 🧲 Move Around (Pan): Hold down Shift and drag the mouse to slide the model around the screen like it’s floating in space! 🌌

💡 Tip: Try zooming in to look at tiny details or spinning fast to see every side — it’s like holding the real thing in your hands!

Have fun exploring and becoming a 3D adventurer! 🧭🌍
