<h4>🎮 How to Move Your Model Around</h4>
<p>Use your mouse or trackpad to control the model like a pro! Here's how:</p>

<ul>

  <li><strong>🔍 Zoom In and Out:</strong> Roll your mouse wheel or slide on your trackpad to zoom super close or zoom out to see the whole thing.</li>
  <li><strong>🧲 Move Around (Pan):</strong> Hold down
